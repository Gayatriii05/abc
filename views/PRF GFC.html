ADVANCED PRP & GFC TREATMENTS

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy

  • Rejuvenate Your Skin: PRP is a revolutionary treatment that uses your body's own platelets to stimulate collagen production, reducing wrinkles, fine lines, and scars.
  • Hair Restoration: Combat hair loss and promote hair growth with PRP therapy, offering a non-surgical solution to thinning hair and balding.

Growth Factor Concentrate (GFC) Treatment

  • Skin Rejuvenation: GFC treatment harnesses the power of growth factors to revitalize your skin, reducing signs of aging and improving texture and tone.
  • Scar Reduction: Say goodbye to acne scars and blemishes with GFC therapy, leaving your skin smoother and more youthful.
