ACNE TREATMENT

Understanding Acne

Acne is a common skin condition that can affect people of all ages. It occurs when hair follicles become clogged with oil, dead skin cells, and bacteria, leading to the formation of pimples, blackheads, and cysts. Acne can be distressing, but it is treatable.

Our Acne Treatment Services

At Skin Elegance Clinic, we offer a range of treatments to address both active acne and acne scars. Dr. Kajol Murade Patil and her team will develop a personalized treatment plan that best suits your skin type and acne severity. Our services include:

  • Acne Consultation: A thorough evaluation to understand your skin's condition and create a customized treatment plan.
  • Topical and Oral Medications: Prescription treatments that target acne both on the surface and from within.
  • Chemical Peels: Specially formulated peels to rejuvenate the skin, reduce acne scarring, and improve overall texture.
  • Microneedling: Stimulate collagen production and reduce acne scars with our advanced microneedling treatments.
  • Laser Therapy: State-of-the-art lasers to target acne-causing bacteria, reduce inflammation, and promote healthier skin.
  • Extraction and Comedone Removal: Safe removal of clogged pores, blackheads, and whiteheads by our experienced team.
  • Skincare Recommendations: A personalized skincare regimen designed to maintain healthy skin and prevent future breakouts.
  • Acne Scar Management: Advanced treatments like dermal fillers, microdermabrasion, and scar revision to reduce the appearance of acne scars.
