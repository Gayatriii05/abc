WART REMOVAL

Understanding Wart Removal

Warts are small, benign growths caused by the human papillomavirus (HPV). They can appear on various parts of the body, including hands, feet, and face. While most warts are harmless, they can be unsightly or cause discomfort. Wart removal treatments can help you regain smooth and clear skin.

Our Wart Removal Services

At Skin Elegance Clinic, we provide safe and effective wart removal options performed by Dr. Kajol Murade Patil. Our clinic offers a range of treatment options, tailored to the size, location, and type of wart. Our services include:

  • Initial Consultation: A thorough assessment to determine the type of wart and the most suitable treatment method.
  • Laser Wart Removal: A non-invasive laser treatment that targets the wart tissue, breaking it down without harming the surrounding skin.
  • Cryotherapy (Freezing): The wart is frozen with liquid nitrogen, causing the tissue to die and fall off over time.
  • Electrosurgery: A technique that uses electrical currents to burn off the wart tissue for precise removal.
  • Cauterization: A method that uses heat to remove the wart and prevent bleeding, suitable for smaller warts.
  • Topical Treatments: Dr. Kajol may recommend topical solutions to be applied at home or during follow-up visits to remove warts.
  • Post-Removal Care: After the procedure, we provide aftercare instructions to ensure proper healing and prevent recurrence of warts.
