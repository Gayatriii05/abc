HAIR TRANSPLANT

At Skin Elegance Clinic, We Understand the Impact of Hair Loss

Hair loss can impact your self-esteem and overall well-being. Dr. Kajol Murade Patil and her team provide cutting-edge hair restoration treatments to help you regain confidence with natural-looking results.

Why Choose Skin Elegance Clinic?

  • Expertise: Dr. Kajol Murade Patil specializes in hair transplant procedures, utilizing the latest techniques.
  • Personalized Plans: We assess your hair loss condition and customize treatments based on your goals.
  • Advanced Techniques: We offer Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) for optimal results.
  • Comfortable Environment: Our Aurangabad clinic ensures a stress-free and positive experience.

Our Hair Transplant Services

We offer comprehensive hair restoration solutions:

  • FUT: A strip of hair-bearing skin is transplanted for maximum graft yield.
  • FUE: Individual follicles are extracted and implanted, leaving minimal scarring.
  • PRP Therapy: Stimulates hair growth and enhances transplant results.
