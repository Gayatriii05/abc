MESOTHERAPY

About Mesotherapy

Mesotherapy is a non-invasive cosmetic procedure that targets various skin concerns and offers a safe and effective way to address a range of issues, including:

  • Skin Rejuvenation: Mesotherapy stimulates collagen and elastin production, enhancing skin elasticity and reducing signs of aging.
  • Hair Restoration: This treatment is also effective for hair loss concerns, promoting hair growth and strengthening hair follicles.
  • Cellulite Reduction: Mesotherapy can help reduce the appearance of cellulite, leaving your skin smoother and more toned.
  • Scar and Stretch Mark Reduction: It can fade scars and stretch marks by promoting tissue regeneration and collagen synthesis.
  • Hyperpigmentation and Acne Scars: Mesotherapy can even out skin tone and improve the appearance of acne scars and pigmentation issues.

What to Expect During a Mesotherapy Session

  • Consultation: Dr. Kajol will assess your skin concerns and medical history to create a personalized treatment plan.
  • Treatment: During the procedure, a series of micro-injections containing a customized cocktail of vitamins, minerals, and other beneficial substances are administered directly into the target area.
  • Minimal Discomfort: Most patients experience minimal discomfort during the procedure, thanks to the use of fine needles and topical anesthesia if needed.
  • Recovery: There is little to no downtime, and you can usually resume your daily activities immediately.
  • Results: Results are typically noticeable after a few sessions, with continued improvement over time.
