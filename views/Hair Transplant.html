Our Hair Transplant Services

We offer a variety of hair restoration treatments, including:

  • Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT): This technique involves removing a strip of hair-bearing skin from the donor area and transplanting it into the thinning or balding areas. FUT is ideal for patients seeking maximum graft yield.
  • Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE): FUE is a minimally invasive method in which individual hair follicles are extracted and transplanted to the target area. This method is popular for its minimal scarring and quicker recovery time.
  • Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy: Enhance your hair transplant results with PRP therapy, which stimulates hair growth and promotes the overall health of your scalp and hair follicles.
