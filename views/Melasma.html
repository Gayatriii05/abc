MELASMA TREATMENT

What is Melasma?

Melasma is a common skin disorder characterized by brown to gray-brown patches on the face, typically occurring on the cheeks, forehead, nose, and upper lip. This condition can be triggered by hormonal changes, sun exposure, genetics, and certain medications. Melasma is more prevalent in women, especially during pregnancy (often referred to as the "mask of pregnancy").
