About Dr. Kajol Murade Patil

Dr. Kajol Murade Patil is a board-certified dermatologist with a passion for helping patients achieve healthy and beautiful skin and hair. She is recognized for her expertise in scalp conditions and is dedicated to providing compassionate care that addresses the root causes of your scalp problems. Dr. Patil stays at the forefront of dermatological advancements, ensuring that her patients receive the most up-to-date treatments.

Services Offered
