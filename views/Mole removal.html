MOLE REMOVAL

Understanding Mole Removal

Moles are common skin growths that can vary in color, shape, and size. While most moles are harmless, some may be a cosmetic concern or require removal due to potential health risks. Mole removal is a safe and effective procedure that can enhance your skin's appearance.

Our Mole Removal Services

At Skin Elegance Clinic, we offer advanced mole removal treatments performed by Dr. Kajol Murade Patil. We take a personalized approach to each mole removal procedure, ensuring the best possible results with minimal discomfort. Our services include:

  • Initial Consultation: A thorough evaluation to determine the best treatment for your mole, whether it's cosmetic or requires medical attention.
  • Laser Mole Removal: A non-invasive procedure using advanced laser technology to remove moles quickly and effectively.
  • Excision Removal: A surgical technique where the mole is cut out and the skin is closed with stitches, used for larger or deeper moles.
  • Cauterization: A method that uses heat to remove the mole and cauterize the area to prevent bleeding, ideal for smaller moles.
  • Biopsy Option: If necessary, Dr. Kajol will send the mole for a biopsy to check for any abnormalities or potential health concerns.
  • Post-Removal Care: We provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure proper healing and avoid complications.
