Medi Facial / Clean Up

Our signature Medi Facial / Clean Up treatment is designed to rejuvenate your skin, leaving it refreshed, radiant, and glowing. Whether you have concerns about acne, uneven skin tone, fine lines, or just want to pamper yourself, our Medi Facial / Clean Up is the perfect solution.

Key Benefits
  • Deep Cleansing: Our expert team will cleanse your skin thoroughly, removing dirt, oil, and impurities to unclog pores.
  • Exfoliation: Gentle exfoliation helps to remove dead skin cells, revealing smoother, more youthful skin.
  • Customized Treatment: Dr. Kajol Murade Patil tailors each treatment to your specific skin type and concerns, ensuring optimal results.
  • Hydration: We replenish your skin with essential nutrients and moisture, leaving it supple and hydrated.
  • Stress Relief: Our relaxing facial massage helps relieve stress, promoting overall well-being.
  • Instant Glow: Walk out of our clinic with a noticeable, immediate improvement in your skin's texture and tone.

Why Choose Skin Elegance Clinic?

  • Expertise: Dr. Kajol Murade Patil is a board-certified dermatologist with a wealth of experience in skincare.
  • Personalized Care: We understand that each person's skin is unique. Our treatments are customized to address your specific concerns and goals.
  • Cutting-Edge Technology: We stay at the forefront of dermatological advancements, using the latest technology and techniques to deliver exceptional results.
  • Compassion and Care: At Skin Elegance Clinic, we prioritize your comfort and well-being throughout your journey with us.
