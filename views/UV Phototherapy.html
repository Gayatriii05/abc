UV PHOTOTHERAPY

(VITILIGO AND PSORIASIS)

UV Phototherapy for Vitiligo

Vitiligo is a skin disorder characterized by the loss of pigmentation in certain areas of the skin, resulting in white patches. Dr. Kajol Murade Patil offers specialized UV phototherapy treatments for Vitiligo. UV phototherapy involves exposing the affected skin to controlled doses of ultraviolet (UV) light. This treatment helps stimulate the production of melanin, the pigment responsible for skin color, and can help even out skin tone.

UV Phototherapy for Psoriasis

Psoriasis is a chronic skin condition that causes the rapid buildup of skin cells, resulting in thick, red, scaly patches. UV phototherapy is an effective treatment option for managing psoriasis. Skin Elegance Clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art UV phototherapy equipment to provide targeted treatment for psoriasis. UV light helps slow down the excessive growth of skin cells, reducing inflammation and improving the appearance of psoriatic lesions.
