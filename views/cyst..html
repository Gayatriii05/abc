CYST REMOVAL

Understanding Cysts

A cyst is a sac-like structure that can form in or on your skin. It may contain fluid, air, or other substances. Cysts can develop in various areas of the body, including the face, neck, back, and scalp. While most cysts are benign, they can become infected, painful, or unsightly, making cyst removal necessary.

Our Cyst Removal Services

At Skin Elegance Clinic, we provide safe and effective cyst removal treatments. Dr. Kajol Murade Patil will assess the size and location of the cyst and recommend the best removal method for your needs. Our services include:

  • Initial Consultation: A thorough examination of the cyst to determine its type, size, and ideal treatment option.
  • Excision: A minor surgical procedure where the cyst is removed along with its capsule to reduce the chances of recurrence.
  • Drainage: For infected or fluid-filled cysts, a drainage procedure may be performed to alleviate discomfort and prevent further issues.
  • Laser Therapy: A less invasive approach using laser technology to target and remove certain types of cysts.
  • Post-Removal Care: After the cyst is removed, we provide detailed care instructions to ensure proper healing and prevent infections.
