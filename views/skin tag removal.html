SKIN TAG REMOVAL

Understanding Skin Tags

Skin tags are benign growths that appear on the skin, typically in areas where the skin folds, such as the neck, underarms, and eyelids. While they are harmless, they can be bothersome and unsightly. Skin tag removal treatments can restore the smooth appearance of your skin and help you feel more confident.

Our Skin Tag Removal Services

At Skin Elegance Clinic, we offer effective and safe treatments for removing skin tags. Dr. Kajol Murade Patil will assess your skin and recommend the most suitable method for your needs. Our services include:

  • Initial Consultation: A thorough evaluation to determine the size, type, and location of your skin tag, as well as the best treatment option for removal.
  • Excision: A simple and effective procedure where the skin tag is carefully removed using a surgical scalpel. This method is ideal for larger skin tags.
  • Cauterization: A technique that uses heat to burn off the skin tag, preventing further bleeding or regrowth.
  • Cryotherapy (Freezing): Freezing the skin tag using liquid nitrogen to destroy its tissue, causing it to fall off naturally.
  • Electrosurgery: This method uses an electrical current to remove the skin tag by cutting it and coagulating the tissue to stop bleeding.
  • Post-Removal Care: After the procedure, we provide instructions for proper care to ensure optimal healing and prevent complications.
